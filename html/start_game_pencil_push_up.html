Pencil Push-Up

Level 1

Follow moving objects with your eyes to improve tracking abilities and coordination.

How To Play

Instructions :

  • Sit comfortably in front of the computer screen and keep your eyes aligned with the center of the display.
  • Focus on the pencil object shown on the screen as it moves slowly toward the center.
  • Maintain visual focus for as long as the image remains clear, without moving your head.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions and repeat the exercise until the session is completed.

Benefits :

  • Improves visual focus and eye coordination by training the eyes to maintain clarity on moving objects.
  • Helps reduce eye strain caused by prolonged screen usage through controlled visual exercises.
  • Enhances concentration and visual endurance.
  • Provides a safe and accessible therapy method.
