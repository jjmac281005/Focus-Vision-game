Bordie String

Level 1

Focus on beads along a string to train eye alignment, convergence, and depth perception.

How To Play

Instructions :

  • Sit comfortably in front of the computer screen and keep your head straight.
  • Hold or view the Brock string so that one end is aligned with the center of your nose.
  • Focus on one bead at a time, starting from the nearest bead and moving toward the farthest.
  • Maintain focus until the image appears clear, then move to the next bead.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions and repeat the exercise until the session is completed.

Benefits :

  • Improves eye convergence and alignment by training both eyes to work together.
  • Helps reduce symptoms of eye fatigue and visual discomfort.
  • Strengthens visual coordination and concentration for reading and near-vision tasks.
  • Provides a simple and effective vision therapy exercise that can be practiced anytime.
