Barrel Cards

Level 1

Focus on barrel shapes to improve eye convergence, divergence, and binocular coordination.

How To Play

Instructions :

  • Sit comfortably in front of the computer screen with your head straight and relaxed.
  • Hold or view the barrel card at eye level, keeping it aligned with the center of your nose.
  • Start by focusing on the largest barrel, making sure the image appears clear and single.
  • Gradually shift your focus to the medium and smallest barrels, following the on-screen guidance.
  • Keep your head still and use only your eyes during the exercise. Repeat the activity until the session is completed.

Benefits :

  • Improves eye convergence and divergence by training the eyes to adjust focus at different distances.
  • Helps reduce eye strain and visual discomfort caused by prolonged near-vision tasks.
  • Enhances binocular vision and eye teamwork for clearer, single vision.
  • Provides a structured and effective vision therapy exercise that can be practiced anytime.
